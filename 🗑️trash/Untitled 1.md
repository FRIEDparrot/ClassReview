$$T_{xx} + T_{yy} = 0$$

$$T(0,y)  = T_{0},   T(\infty, y) = T(x, 0) = T(x, H) = 0$$

$$\eta(x,y) =\frac{\sin\left(\frac{\pi}{H} y \right)}{ \sinh  \frac{\pi}{H} x}$$
并满足 $f(0) = 0, f(\infty) = T_0$
