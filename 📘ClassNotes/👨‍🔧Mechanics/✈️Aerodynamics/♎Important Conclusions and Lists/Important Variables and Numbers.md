Reynolds number
$$Re = \frac{\rho_\infty V_\infty c}{\mu_\infty}$$
Mach Number
$$M_\infty = \frac{V_\infty}{a_\infty}$$
Prandtl Number: 
$$Pr=\frac{\mu_\infty c_p}{k_\infty}$$
sound speed: 
$$a_\infty = \sqrt{\kappa \rho v} =\sqrt{\kappa\frac{p}{\rho}} =\sqrt{\kappa RT}$$
where $\kappa =1.4$ (for steam)
